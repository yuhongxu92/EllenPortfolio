Problems and Solutions

  • Problems:
  • How to Foster User Achievement?
  • How to Enable Quick Access to Entry Points?
  • How to Provide Post-Planting Feedback and Tracking?
  • User Concern: Affordability of Planting
  • User Desire: Understanding Benefits of Bamboo Purchase
  • Building Trust in the Company: Seeking User Reviews
  • Solutionsï¼š
  • Issue shareable e-certificates for user achievements.
  • Use clear buttons for easy navigation, with pricing and planting rationale labels.
  • Send a post-planting email with a map link for location tracking.
  • Provide transparent pricing for different bamboo varieties.
  • Offer comprehensive content on bamboo benefits, emphasizing sustainability.
  • Include a website link for company profile and user testimonials to build trust.

Results:

  • After implementing the changes, there was a 20% increase in the quantity of bamboo purchased by users.
  • Additionally, there was a 15% rise in the number of users visiting the official website. This demonstrates a significant improvement in user engagement and conversion rates.
